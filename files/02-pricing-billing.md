# GymSathi — Pricing & Billing

## What are the pricing plans?

GymSathi has three plans:

### BASIC — NPR 800/month
- Up to 100 members
- Manual attendance tracking
- Payment tracking
- Core dashboard

### STANDARD — NPR 1,200/month ⭐ (Best Value)
- Up to 300 members
- WhatsApp renewal reminders (automated)
- One-tap attendance
- PDF report exports
- Everything in Basic

### PREMIUM — NPR 2,000/month
- Unlimited members
- Multi-staff access (multiple logins)
- Advanced analytics
- Priority support
- Everything in Standard

## Which plan should I choose?

- **New/Small gym (under 100 members):** Start with Basic
- **Growing gym (100–300 members):** Standard is the best value — WhatsApp automation alone saves hours every week
- **Large facility or multiple branches:** Premium with unlimited members and multi-staff access

## Is there a free trial?

Yes! All plans include a **30-day free trial** with full access. No credit card required to start.

## How do I pay for GymSathi?

You can pay using:
- **eSewa** (Nepal's most popular digital wallet)
- **Khalti** digital wallet
- Credit/debit cards

## Are subscription fees refundable?

Fees are billed in advance. Refunds are only available as stated in our refund policy. Please contact support for specific refund requests.

## What happens if I miss a payment?

If a subscription payment fails, we will notify you before taking any action. Continued non-payment may result in temporary suspension of your account.

## Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades are effective immediately. Contact support for plan changes.

## Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. The monthly subscription price listed is what you pay. WhatsApp message costs may vary depending on your messaging gateway provider.
