# GymSathi — General FAQ

## What is GymSathi?

GymSathi is a comprehensive gym management SaaS (Software as a Service) platform built specifically for Nepal's fitness industry. It helps fitness facility owners automate their daily operations — member management, payment tracking, attendance, and renewal reminders — all in one place.

**Tagline:** "Stop the chaos. Start the growth."

## Who is GymSathi for?

GymSathi is designed for:
- Fitness Clubs & Gyms
- Dance Academies
- Yoga Centers
- Swim Centers
- Spas & Wellness Centers
- Training Institutes
- Any kinetic/fitness facility in Nepal

## What problems does GymSathi solve?

- Eliminates manual, paper-based member registers
- Stops chasing payments manually — automated reminders do it for you
- Tracks attendance digitally with one tap
- Sends automatic WhatsApp renewal reminders to members
- Manages revenue in NPR using local payment gateways (eSewa & Khalti)
- Provides real-time analytics and reports

## Is GymSathi made for Nepal?

Yes. GymSathi is built specifically for Nepal's market:
- Supports eSewa and Khalti (Nepal's most popular digital wallets)
- Works in Nepali Rupees (NPR)
- WhatsApp and SMS integration for local communication
- Support available in Nepali, English, and Hindi

## How many facilities are using GymSathi?

GymSathi currently serves 50+ facilities across Nepal.

## What is "Kinetic" in GymSathi?

"Kinetic" represents energy in motion. Just like a gym is a dynamic, moving environment, GymSathi keeps your business moving forward. It handles static admin tasks so you can focus on growth and member transformation.
