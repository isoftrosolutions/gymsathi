# GymSathi — Troubleshooting & Common Issues

## I can't log in to my account

**Step-by-step fix:**
1. Confirm you're using the correct registered email
2. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page to reset
3. Check spam/junk folder for the password reset email
4. Try a different browser or clear cache
5. If still stuck: call +977 9811144402 or email mind59024@gmail.com

## I'm not receiving WhatsApp reminders

Possible reasons:
- WhatsApp integration may not be configured — go to Settings > Notifications
- Member's phone number may be missing or incorrect
- The reminder timing may not have been triggered yet (set to X days before expiry)
- WhatsApp gateway may be temporarily down

Fix: Check Settings > Notifications and verify the member's phone number is saved correctly. Contact support if the problem persists.

## A member's payment is not showing up

1. Check the payment was actually completed (check eSewa/Khalti transaction history)
2. Go to the member's profile and check their payment history
3. Manually record the payment if it was done in cash
4. For gateway payments not syncing, contact support with the transaction ID

## I can't export reports / PDF not downloading

1. Check your browser is not blocking downloads (allow pop-ups for GymSathi)
2. Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
3. Make sure you're on Standard or Premium plan (PDF exports not available on Basic)
4. Contact support if the issue continues

## The dashboard is loading slowly

1. Check your internet connection
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Try a different browser
4. If the issue persists, it may be a server-side issue — contact support or check if maintenance is happening

## A member's attendance is not being marked

1. Make sure you're on Standard or Premium plan for one-tap attendance
2. Search the correct member profile before marking
3. Check the date/time settings on your device
4. Contact support if attendance records seem incorrect

## I accidentally deleted a member — can I recover?

Contact support immediately at mind59024@gmail.com or +977 9811144402. We may be able to restore from backup within a short window.

## WhatsApp messages are going to the wrong numbers

1. Check the member's profile — update their phone number
2. Ensure phone numbers are stored with country code (+977 for Nepal)
3. Go to Settings > Notifications and test the integration

## I'm getting a payment error when trying to subscribe

1. Ensure your eSewa/Khalti account has sufficient balance
2. Try a different payment method
3. Check if your bank/wallet has any restrictions
4. Contact support with the error message/screenshot

## Staff members can't access certain features

Staff access is limited by their role. If a staff member needs more access:
1. Go to Settings > Staff Management
2. Find the staff member and edit their role/permissions
3. Save changes

Note: Multi-staff access requires the Premium plan.
