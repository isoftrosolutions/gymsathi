# GymSathi — Sectors & Use Cases

## Which types of facilities does GymSathi support?

GymSathi is designed for any kinetic (movement-based) fitness facility:

### Fitness Clubs & Gyms
- Track members, monthly fees, attendance
- Automated renewal reminders via WhatsApp
- Revenue dashboards to see daily/monthly income
- Manage personal training packages

### Dance Academies
- Student/member management by batch or class
- Fee tracking per student
- Attendance per class/session
- WhatsApp reminders for fee dues

### Yoga Centers
- Manage regular members and drop-in visitors
- Track class attendance
- Automate monthly renewal messages
- Simple dashboard for small centers

### Swim Centers
- Member and session-based tracking
- Pool attendance logs
- Fee management with eSewa/Khalti
- Renewal reminders

### Spas & Wellness Centers
- Manage membership and packages
- Client visit tracking
- Revenue and service reports
- Automated re-engagement messages

### Training Institutes
- Student enrollment management
- Course/batch fee tracking
- Attendance records
- Automated communication

## Is GymSathi only for large gyms?

No. GymSathi works for all sizes:
- **Small gyms/studios** → Basic plan (NPR 800/mo, up to 100 members)
- **Mid-size gyms** → Standard plan (NPR 1,200/mo, up to 300 members)
- **Large gyms/multi-branch** → Premium plan (NPR 2,000/mo, unlimited members)

## I run a small yoga center with 30 students. Is GymSathi right for me?

Absolutely. The Basic plan at NPR 800/month covers up to 100 members and handles fee tracking and attendance. As you grow, you can upgrade to Standard to get automated WhatsApp reminders — which is especially useful to avoid students missing renewal dates.
