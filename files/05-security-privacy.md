# GymSathi — Security & Privacy

## How does GymSathi protect my data?

GymSathi implements multiple layers of security:

### End-to-End Encryption
- All communication between your device and our servers is encrypted using **256-bit SSL (TLS)**
- Your member data, payment history, and staff records are protected both in transit and at rest

### Daily Backups
- Automated daily backups of all facility databases
- In case of a critical failure, data can be restored within minutes
- Ensures minimal disruption to your business

### Role-Based Access Control
- You control who sees what
- Set permissions for each staff member — they only access modules relevant to their role
- Nothing more, nothing less

## Who can see my member data?

Only you and the staff members you authorize can access your data. GymSathi employees do not access your data except when required for technical support and with your permission.

## Is member payment data safe?

Yes. Payment processing is handled through certified payment gateways (eSewa, Khalti). GymSathi does not store raw payment card data.

## Do you sell my data to third parties?

No. GymSathi does not sell your data or your members' data to any third party. Your data is yours.

## Who owns my gym's data?

You retain full ownership of all data you input into the GymSathi platform. GymSathi only processes it to deliver the service as described in our Terms of Service.

## What is your privacy policy?

GymSathi collects and processes data only as necessary to deliver the service. We do not use your data for advertising or share it with unauthorized third parties.

## What happens to my data if I stop using GymSathi?

Upon account termination, you can export all your data before cancelling. GymSathi retains data for a period required by applicable law, after which it is deleted.

## I found a security vulnerability. What should I do?

We take security very seriously. If you've discovered a bug or security issue:
- Contact us immediately via our Contact Support page
- Email: mind59024@gmail.com
- We will investigate and respond promptly

Please do not publicly disclose the vulnerability before we have a chance to fix it.

## Is GymSathi compliant with data protection laws?

GymSathi follows responsible data handling practices in accordance with applicable Nepali laws and general best practices for data protection.

## Can I delete a member's personal data?

Yes, as the facility owner, you can delete member profiles from your dashboard. Deleted data is removed from active storage.
