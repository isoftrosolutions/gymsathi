# GymSathi — Features & Usage

## Core Features

### 1. Digital Member Management (Digital Notebook)
- Add, search, and manage all members digitally
- Full member history at your fingertips
- No more paper registers or lost records
- Store member photos, contact info, join date, plan details

### 2. WhatsApp Pulse (Automated Reminders)
- Automatically send WhatsApp messages to members before renewal
- Birthday wishes sent automatically
- Custom notification messages
- 100% automated — set it up once, runs forever
- Available on Standard and Premium plans

### 3. Attendance Tracking
- **Basic plan**: Manual attendance logging
- **Standard/Premium**: One-tap attendance — scan or tap to mark attendance instantly
- View attendance history per member
- Identify inactive members easily

### 4. Payment & Revenue Tracking
- Track all member payments in NPR
- Know exactly who has paid and who hasn't
- Integrated with eSewa and Khalti payment gateways
- Payment history stored per member

### 5. Reporting & Analytics
- PDF report exports (Standard and above)
- Revenue reports
- Membership reports
- Attendance analytics
- **Advanced analytics** available on Premium plan

### 6. Multi-Staff Access (Premium)
- Add multiple staff/receptionist logins
- Role-based permissions — staff see only what they need
- Track who made what change

### 7. Multi-Location Management
- Manage multiple branches from one account
- Contact us for multi-location pricing

## How do I add a new member?

1. Go to the **Members** section in your dashboard
2. Click **"Add Member"**
3. Fill in their name, contact, photo, and membership plan
4. Save — the member is automatically tracked in the system

## How do I track attendance?

- **Basic**: Go to Attendance, find the member, mark manually
- **Standard/Premium**: Use one-tap attendance — find member and tap to mark instantly

## How do WhatsApp reminders work?

GymSathi automatically sends WhatsApp messages to members when their membership is about to expire. You configure the timing (e.g., 7 days before expiry) and the message template, and the system sends it without any manual work.

## How do I export reports?

Go to **Reports > Export Data** in your dashboard. You can export:
- Member lists
- Attendance records
- Payment history
- Format: CSV or Excel (and PDF for membership reports)

## Can I track multiple membership plans?

Yes. You can create multiple membership plans (monthly, quarterly, yearly, etc.) with different pricing, and assign them to individual members.

## What payment gateways are supported?

- **eSewa** — Nepal's #1 digital wallet
- **Khalti** — popular among youth in Nepal
- Credit/debit cards

## Can members pay online through the system?

Yes, members can pay via eSewa or Khalti through the platform. Payment is recorded automatically.

## Does GymSathi work on mobile?

Yes, the GymSathi dashboard is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. Staff can manage the gym from any smartphone or tablet.

## What is "Local Core"?

Local Core means GymSathi is built around Nepal's payment ecosystem — eSewa and Khalti are natively integrated, billing is in NPR, and no foreign currency or payment hassle.

## Can I send SMS notifications?

GymSathi supports SMS/WhatsApp gateway integration for notifications. Contact support for SMS setup details.
