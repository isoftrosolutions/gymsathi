# GymSathi — Account & Authentication

## How do I create an account?

1. Visit the GymSathi website
2. Click **"Start Free Trial"**
3. Enter your email address
4. Complete the registration form with your facility details
5. Your 30-day free trial starts immediately

## How do I log in?

Visit the GymSathi website and click the **Login** button. Enter your registered email and password.

## How do I reset my password?

1. Go to the login page
2. Click **"Forgot Password"**
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your email for a reset link
5. Click the link and set a new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam/junk folder or contact support at mind59024@gmail.com.

## Can I have multiple staff logins?

Yes, on the **Premium plan**, you can add multiple staff members with separate logins. Each staff member gets role-based access — they only see what they're permitted to.

On Basic and Standard plans, there is a single owner login. Upgrade to Premium for multi-staff access.

## How do I add a staff member? (Premium)

1. Go to **Settings > Staff Management**
2. Click **"Add Staff"**
3. Enter their email and set their role/permissions
4. They'll receive an invitation email to set up their login

## What roles can staff have?

Staff roles include different access levels:
- **Owner/Admin**: Full access to everything
- **Staff/Receptionist**: Can add members, mark attendance, and record payments
- **Viewer**: Read-only access to reports

## How do I update my account information?

Go to **Settings > Profile** in your dashboard to update your name, email, phone number, and facility details.

## How do I change my subscription plan?

Go to **Settings > Billing** or contact our support team at mind59024@gmail.com or +977 9811144402.

## What happens to my data if I cancel?

Upon cancellation, your access stops at the end of your billing period. Your data is retained for a reasonable period as required by law, after which it may be deleted. Export your data before cancelling using the Reports export feature.

## How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, contact us at mind59024@gmail.com. Please export all your data before requesting deletion.

## I can't log in — what should I do?

1. Make sure you're using the correct email address
2. Try resetting your password via "Forgot Password"
3. Clear your browser cache and try again
4. If still unable to log in, contact support:
   - Email: mind59024@gmail.com
   - Phone: +977 9811144402

## Is my account secure?

Yes. GymSathi uses:
- 256-bit SSL encryption for all connections
- Secure password hashing
- Role-based access control
- Session management and automatic logout

## Can I use GymSathi on multiple devices?

Yes, you can log in from any device with a web browser — desktop, tablet, or mobile.
